Introduction to Digital Marketing

Digital marketing encompasses all marketing efforts that use the internet and electronic devices. As consumers spend increasing time online, businesses must establish strong digital presences to reach their target audiences. Digital marketing offers measurable, cost-effective strategies for brand building, customer acquisition, and engagement.

Social Media Marketing

Social media platforms provide powerful tools for brand communication and community building. Students learn to develop social media strategies, create engaging content, manage brand communities, and measure social media performance. Platforms covered include Instagram, LinkedIn, TikTok, and Facebook, each with unique audience characteristics and content formats.

Search Engine Optimization

SEO is the practice of optimizing websites to rank higher in search engine results. Students learn on-page optimization including keyword research, meta tags, and content structure. Off-page strategies cover link building, domain authority, and local SEO. Technical SEO addresses site speed, mobile responsiveness, and structured data.

Content Marketing Strategy

Content marketing focuses on creating valuable, relevant content to attract and retain audiences. Students learn to develop content strategies, create blog posts, videos, infographics, and email campaigns. Content planning, editorial calendars, and repurposing techniques help maintain consistent brand messaging across channels.

Marketing Analytics and Measurement

Data-driven marketing requires understanding key performance indicators and analytics tools. Students learn to use Google Analytics, track conversion funnels, measure campaign ROI, and create performance reports. A/B testing and data interpretation skills help optimize marketing strategies for better results.

Brand Building and Digital Strategy

The course concludes with integrated digital marketing strategy development. Students learn brand positioning, competitive analysis, customer journey mapping, and multichannel campaign planning. A final project requires creating a comprehensive digital marketing plan for a real or simulated business.
